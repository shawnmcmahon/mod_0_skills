class: entree

attributes:
temperature (integer)
menuType (string)
isServed (boolean)
tableNumber (integer)

methods:
grill_entree (Cooks the entree to the appropriate temperature
value.)
close_menu (Used to end the breakfast or lunch
menus so only appropriate entrees are served.)
serve_entree (Confirms the entree has been served to the
customer by referencing the status of the isServed boolean.)
clean_table (Uses the tableNumber attribute to reference
which table needs to have the plates of entrees removed
from the previous customers.)
